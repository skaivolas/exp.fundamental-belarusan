Вясельная песня [Туман явар...]

Marriage Song [Sycamore fog...]

Fog sycamore, valley sycamore, (2x)
Behind the fog, nothing can be seen. (2x)
Only the green oak can be seen, (2x)
Under that oak stood a guelder rose, (2x)
From under the rose a maiden drew water, (2x)
She dropped a golden ring, (2x)
And who gets that ring,
The same will have me on his hand.
This little story happened, (2x)
And I will get that ring,
I will have you on my hand. (2x)

(Zhuk A.S., 72 years old, Yaremichy village)
