Article "Why do we call it so?" from 1979

...The village Bautsichy of the Yaremichy village soviet has its own legend, which is related to the invasion of the swedes into Poland and Russia in the beginning of the 18th century.  On this spot there was a swamp, and when the swedes passed over it, they "boutalisya*."  Many of them perished.

*splashed
